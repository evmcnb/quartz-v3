## Symptoms

[[Nephrotic syndrome]]

- oedema
- hypoalbuminemia
- proteinuria >3.5g/day

[[Nephritic syndrome]]

- [[Acute Kidney Injury]]
- **haematuria** present
- proteinuria (non-nephrotic)
- hypertension
- Post-streptococcal
- Vasculitis

## Signs

- Frothy urine

## Diagnostic tests

- Urinalysis
  - haematuria and/or proteinuria
- Immunological investigations
- Ultrasound
- Biopsy
