Emphysema and chronic bronchitis are two conditions that make up chronic obstructive pulmonary disease ([[COPD]])

## Causes/Factors

- Tobacco/marijuana smoke
- Air pollution
- Chemical fumes & dust

## Symptoms

- Gradual onset dyspnea
- Productive cough
- Frequent chest infections
- Wheeze

## Signs

- Tachypnoea
