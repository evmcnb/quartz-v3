## Fluroquinolones
_Ciprofloxacin, levofloxacin, moxifloxacin_
- Broad spectrum including _Pseudomonas_
- CNS effects: $\downarrow$ seizure threshold, headache, drowsiness, mood change
- Peripheral neuropathy 
- $\uparrow$ QT
